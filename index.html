About Me

I am currently a fourth year PhD student in electrical engineering at Stanford University and an intern at Grail. I am interested in data-related problems, and ultimately I hope to create something impactful that lies at the intersection of machine learning and genomics. I am a member of David Tse's group, and my research revolves around analysis of single-cell RNA-Seq datasets and understanding deep learning models.

I have also had the privilege of working for some wonderful organizations during my graduate and undergraduate careers including Cellular Research, MC10, MIT Lincoln Laboratory, and Dana-Farber. Please see my CV for more details.
